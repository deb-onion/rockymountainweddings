Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our wedding planning services

How far in advance should I book your services?

For full planning services, we recommend booking 12-18 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (June-September). For day-of coordination, 6-8 months is ideal, though we occasionally have availability for shorter timelines.

Do you only work in specific venues?

While we have established relationships with many venues throughout the Canadian Rockies, we're happy to work at any venue. If it's a location we haven't worked at before, we'll conduct a site visit to ensure we're familiar with all logistics before your wedding day.

What makes mountain weddings different from planning elsewhere?

Mountain weddings require special considerations including weather contingency plans, transportation logistics (especially for remote locations), altitude adjustments for guests, and understanding of local regulations for National Park venues. Our expertise in these areas ensures a smooth planning process.

How do you handle inclement weather for mountain weddings?

We always prepare detailed weather contingency plans for every wedding, especially in the mountains where conditions can change quickly. This includes securing backup indoor locations, arranging appropriate guest transportation, and having timing flexibility built into your wedding day schedule.

What is your payment schedule?

We typically require a 30% deposit to secure your date, with the remaining balance split into 2-3 installments leading up to your wedding. We're happy to work with you to create a payment schedule that fits your needs.
