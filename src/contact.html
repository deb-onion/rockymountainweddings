Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our wedding planning services

When should we start planning our wedding?

For Rocky Mountain weddings, we recommend starting the planning process 12-18 months before your desired date, especially if you're considering popular venues which book up quickly.

Do you offer customized planning packages?

Absolutely! While we have standard packages to meet common needs, we understand every wedding is unique. We can create a custom package tailored specifically to your requirements.

What's your typical process for working with couples?

We start with a free consultation to understand your vision, followed by a customized proposal. Once we begin working together, we'll guide you through each phase of planning with regular check-ins.

Do you work with couples from outside the area?

Yes! We specialize in helping couples plan destination weddings in the Canadian Rockies, using a combination of virtual meetings and in-person sessions when possible.
