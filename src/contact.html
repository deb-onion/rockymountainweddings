Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking 12-18 months in advance for full planning services, especially if you're planning a wedding during peak season (June-September). For month-of coordination, 6-8 months in advance is ideal.

Do you only work in the Canadian Rockies?

While we specialize in the Canadian Rockies (Banff, Canmore, Lake Louise, Jasper, etc.), we occasionally take on weddings in other scenic locations. Contact us to discuss your specific location needs.

What's the difference between full planning and month-of coordination?

Full planning includes everything from venue selection and vendor management to design concepts and day-of coordination. Month-of coordination focuses on finalizing details, creating timelines, and managing your wedding day, assuming you've done most of the planning yourself.

Can you help with obtaining permits for outdoor ceremonies?

Yes! Many locations in the Canadian Rockies require special permits for ceremonies. We're familiar with the requirements and can handle the permit application process for you.

Do you offer elopement packages?

Absolutely! We offer specialized elopement packages for couples seeking an intimate celebration in the mountains, including location scouting, permit acquisition, and coordination of essential vendors.

What happens if there's bad weather on our wedding day?

We always create detailed weather contingency plans for every wedding we coordinate. This includes securing backup indoor locations or tent options, and having a clear timeline for making weather-related decisions.
