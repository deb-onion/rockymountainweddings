900A Harvie Heights Rd, Bighorn No. 8, AB T1W 2W2, Canada

Capacity: 20-150 guests
Price Range: $$-$$$
Available: Year-round

The Gem offers a unique mountain venue experience combining rustic charm with modern amenities. Located just minutes from Banff, this hidden treasure provides panoramic mountain views and a private, intimate setting for your celebration.

Fairmont Banff Springs Hotel

A castle in the mountains for your fairy tale wedding

405 Spray Ave, Banff, AB T1L 1J4, Canada

Capacity: 10-800 guests
Price Range: $$$-$$$$
Available: Year-round

Known as Canada's "Castle in the Rockies," the Fairmont Banff Springs offers a blend of old-world charm and modern luxury. With multiple ceremony and reception spaces including grand ballrooms and intimate terraces, this venue accommodates both lavish celebrations and smaller gatherings.

The historic architecture, breathtaking mountain views, and impeccable service make this iconic hotel one of the most sought-after wedding venues in the Canadian Rockies.

On-site Accommodations In-house Catering Indoor & Outdoor Options Historic Property Spa Services
