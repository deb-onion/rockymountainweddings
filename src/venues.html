Banff Wedding Venues

Iconic venues in Canada's first national park

Fairmont Banff Springs

A castle in the mountains for your fairy tale wedding

Capacity: 10-800 guests
Price Range: $$$-$$$$
Available: Year-round

Known as Canada's "Castle in the Rockies," the Fairmont Banff Springs offers a perfect blend of old-world charm and modern luxury. With multiple ceremony and reception spaces including grand ballrooms, intimate terraces, and beautiful outdoor locations, this venue can accommodate both grand celebrations and intimate gatherings.

The historic architecture, stunning mountain views, and impeccable service make this iconic hotel one of the most sought-after wedding venues in the Canadian Rockies.

On-site Accommodations In-house Catering Indoor & Outdoor Options Spa Services Wedding Packages

Tunnel Mountain Reservoir

A stunning outdoor ceremony location with panoramic mountain views

Capacity: Up to 100 guests
Price Range: $-$$
Available: May-October

For couples seeking a beautiful outdoor ceremony location, Tunnel Mountain Reservoir offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and the town of Banff. This semi-private location provides a natural, rustic setting for your ceremony.

This venue is perfect for intimate ceremonies and is often paired with a reception at another Banff location. The spectacular mountain backdrop creates unforgettable photo opportunities.

Outdoor Only Ceremony Site Mountain Views Park Permit Required

Silvertip Resort

Luxury mountain golf resort with panoramic Three Sisters views

Capacity: 20-250 guests
Price Range: $$$-$$$$
Available: Year-round

Silvertip Resort offers a spectacular mountain setting with unobstructed views of the iconic Three Sisters peaks. The venue features multiple ceremony locations including an outdoor pavilion and an elegant indoor space, both with stunning panoramic views.

The spacious reception area features floor-to-ceiling windows, a grand stone fireplace, and a beautiful outdoor terrace. The professional staff and customizable wedding packages make planning your mountain celebration seamless.
